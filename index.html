About Me

Hi! I'm Hugo Michel - AKA Sumishi, I'm 21.
I'm a french video game programmer, mainly interested in game engine programming, tools and procedural generation but I also love to experimenting all kinds of stuffs retated to programming for fun or just out of curiosity.

I started programming in 2020 at Creajeux and never stopped since. During my study, I made several cool projects, alone or with a team, last one being Gecko Party.

I enjoy playing video games, my favorite genre being 2D platformers, no matter the difficulty.
